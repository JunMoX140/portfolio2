Hi! My name is Danever. A career shifter diving into the world of full-stack web development. Journey with me as I explore into the vast yet exciting tech space!

About Me

Traveling in the vast tech space requires a companion that you are familiar with. So, here are a few quick facts about me to get us started:

John B. Lacson Foundation Maritime University-Molo

Having able to pass the rigid scholarship screening, I took the Bachelor’s Degree in Marine Engineering and was able to study for free. I feel like this was the pivotal part of this story. At the time, I thought about taking an I.T. course but due to financial constraints I eventually chose to take the one that gave me a chance to study for free. That said, my educational background is really focused on the technical side. The nearest experience I have to programming during my college days was when he did excel formulas. Despite all this, the fascination and the drive to learn about tech has never died out in me.

On-Board as A Seafarer

After a couple of years as a cadet, I obtained my license and was eventually promoted as a junior officer onboard international going merchant ships. The work is hard but the pay is better. And then, the pandemic came. I was able to go back to work even during the height of the pandemic but it made me think about a lot of stuff. Make no mistake, I love my job as a marine engineer but there seemed to be something that is missing.

The Career Shift

Fast forward to today. Here I am making my first ever personal website. Just two weeks into web development and I was able to unlock new skills. Thanks to Stacktrek and DICT-Region 6, I was given a chance to pursue something that has always been after my own heart. I know I still have a lot to learn. And there will be many challenges. That is why I likened this career shift into a journey into space. An adventure into the unknown. Scary? Very! But a wise man once said; To infinity… and beyond!
